How to Recognize a Leak?

Identifying a leak early can drastically reduce potential damage. Here are some signs to watch out for:

  • Unexpected Water Bills: A sudden spike in your water bill may indicate a hidden leak.
  • Damp Spots and Water Stains: Noticeable damp areas on walls or ceilings are clear signs of a water leak.
  • Reduced Water Pressure: A decrease in water pressure can be indicative of leaks in your plumbing system.
  • Mold and Mildew: The presence of mold or a musty odor often accompanies persistent leaks.
  • Sound of Running Water: Hearing water flow when all faucets are turned off could signal a leak.
