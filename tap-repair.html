Common Tap Issues We Address

Even minor tap issues can lead to significant problems if left unresolved. Here are some common tap problems we handle:

  • Leaking Taps: A persistent drip can significantly increase your water bill and lead to water wastage.
  • Broken Handles: Stiff or broken handles are often a sign of underlying issues.
  • Noise Issues: Unusual sounds like whistling or clanking when you turn on a tap may indicate the need for repair.
  • Water Pressure Fluctuations: If you are experiencing fluctuating water pressure, it could be a valve issue that needs urgent attention.
  • Rust and Corrosion: This not only affects the appearance of your taps but can also lead to further damage if not addressed.
