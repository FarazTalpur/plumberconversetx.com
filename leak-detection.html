  • Gas Line Leaks: Extremely dangerous, gas leaks need immediate attention to ensure safety.

Infrared Thermal Imaging

Infrared thermal imaging is a non-invasive technique used to locate leaks in both residential and commercial properties. This technology detects temperature variations caused by moisture, highlighting areas that require further investigation.

Acoustic Leak Detection

Acoustic leak detection utilizes sensitive equipment to pick up sound differences caused by water escaping through pipes or fissures. This method is effective in pinpointing leaks that may not be visible or accessible.

Pressure Testing

Pressure testing is conducted to assess the integrity of plumbing systems. By pressurizing pipes and examining potential weak points, experts can prevent future leaks and ensure systems operate at optimal performance levels.
