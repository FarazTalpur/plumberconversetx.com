How to Prevent Pipe Problems in Your Converse Property

While we are always ready to provide emergency pipe repair services, we also believe in empowering our customers with knowledge to prevent future issues. Here are some preventative measures you can take:

  • Regular Maintenance: Schedule regular plumbing inspections to detect and address minor issues before they escalate.
  • Proper Waste Disposal: Avoid flushing non-degradable items down the toilet or pouring grease down the drain to prevent clogs.
  • Monitor Water Pressure: High water pressure can cause stress on your pipes, leading to leaks and bursts.

  • Address Leaks Immediately: If you notice signs of a leak, such as water stains or dampness, contact a professional plumber immediately.
